Planned Progression System

Instinct Tree
Long-Term Build Identity

A planned post-launch progression layer designed to deepen player identity across combat, crafting, pets, and faction play — without replacing the importance of gear, forging, or the world itself.

Alpha Husky is being built as a world with long memory, stronger player identity, and deeper reasons to return. Instinct Tree expands that vision through specialization: not by overpowering existing systems, but by reinforcing them. The goal is not to replace progression already earned through gear, pets, forging, and faction presence — but to give players a clearer build path inside the world they are already shaping.
