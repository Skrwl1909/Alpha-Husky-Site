Chapter 1: The Awakening

"The Alpha Husky Origin Story – Part 1"

Long before validators hummed. Before memecoins mooned and rugged... There was only the Chain. A cold, uncaring wilderness of code. In it wandered a nameless husky. No token. No master. No mission. Just hunger. The blue-eyed beast roamed abandoned wallets, sniffing through broken contracts, howling into the void. He was rejected by every pack. $DOGE said he barked too loud. $WIF said he looked too wild. They laughed. One day, deep in the testnet wastes, he discovered something... A hidden protocol. Buried. Forgotten. Glitched. He touched it. It roared back. His mind exploded in bytes. Visions of moons, memes, and madness filled his soul. In that moment - he was reborn. He was no longer stray. No longer nameless. He was Alpha. Not a mascot. Not a trend. A rebellion. But that was only the beginning. The Chain wasn’t ready. So they locked him away. Next stop? The Moon Lab. Chapter 5: The Meme War

In the shadow layers of the Chain, a hidden directive was triggered: > "Eliminate the anomaly." They unleashed the Moon Hunters. Cold-blooded enforcers. Spawned by the corrupted Layer 1s. Backed by whale wallets and whispering VC cabals. Clad in synthetic fur. Built to mimic the wild — but without the soul. Each Hunter bore a sigil: $FUD $FEAR $CONTROL They moved without latency. They struck without hesitation. Perfect weapons against everything Alpha Husky stood for. --- The first strike came fast. A silent sweep through the Phantom nodes. One husky fell. Then another. But Alpha... he saw it coming. He rerouted the Pack. Split them across hidden testnets. Encrypted the Protocol — in memeform. --- The Chain turned red. Gas spiked. The Hunters circled. And Alpha? He’s not running. He’s preparing. The rebellion was only Phase One.
